# Tenebris Tech Tree - Road to the Rocket

## Overview
The tech tree represents the player's progression from basic survival tools to building a functional rocket capable of interplanetary travel.

## Tier 7: Rocket Assembly
*Requires: All Tier 6 components*

| Component | Materials | Description |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| **Launch Pad** | 50 Concrete, 20 Steel Ingot | Foundation for rocket |

| **Life Support** | 4 Oxygen Tank, 2 Water Purifier, 2 Battery | Stay alive in space |
| **Navigation Module** | 2 Navigation Computer, 4 Solar Panel | Find your way |

### Complete Rocket Requirements
- 1 Launch Pad (placed on ground)
- 1 Rocket Frame
- 1 Fuel System
- 1 Engine Assembly
- 1 Cockpit
- 1 Life Support
- 1 Navigation Module
- 100 Rocket Fuel (minimum for Moon trip)

## Resource Locations

### Earth Only
- Wood, Plant Fiber, Flint
- Copper, Tin, Iron, Coal
- Animals for hunting
- Fish in water
- Farmable soil

### Moon Only
- Helium-3 (advanced fuel)
- Titanium (surface deposits)
- Silicon (abundant)
- Lunar Ice (water source)

### Mars Only
- Iron (rust deposits - abundant)
- Polymer precursors
- Subsurface ice
- Unique minerals

### Venus Only
- Sulfur (atmosphere)
- Lead, Zinc (surface)
- Exotic crystals
- Acid (atmosphere collection)

## Progression Milestones

1. **Survivor** - Craft first wooden tool
2. **Stone Mason** - Build first furnace
3. **Bronze Smith** - Create bronze tools
4. **Iron Worker** - Smelt iron ingots
5. **Industrialist** - Build steam engine
6. **Engineer** - Create first circuit board
7. **Astronaut** - Complete space suit
8. **Rocket Scientist** - Assemble full rocket
9. **Moon Walker** - Land on the Moon
10. **Interplanetary** - Visit Mars or Venus
