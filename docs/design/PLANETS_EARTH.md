# Earth - Home Planet

## Overview
Earth is the starting planet and the most hospitable world in the solar system. It has breathable atmosphere, abundant water, diverse life, and all basic resources needed to begin the journey to space.

**Radius:** 100 units
**Gravity:** 1.0x (baseline)
**Atmosphere:** Breathable (no suit required)
**Day/Night Cycle:** Yes
**Water:** Abundant oceans and lakes

---

## Biomes

### Grasslands
- Rolling hills covered in grass
- Common trees and bushes
- Most animals found here
- Best for early farming

### Forests
- Dense tree coverage
- Abundant wood resources
- Mushrooms and berries
- Deer, rabbits, wolves

### Mountains
- High elevation, rocky terrain
- Rich ore deposits
- Mountain goats, eagles
- Cave entrances

### Deserts
- Sandy terrain, cacti
- Scorpions, snakes
- Hidden oases
- Glass sand for smelting

### Oceans
- Deep water bodies
- Fish, whales, sharks
- Coral, kelp
- Sunken treasures

### Beaches
- Coastal areas
- Crabs, seagulls
- Shells, driftwood
- Sea turtles nest here

## Weather

| Weather | Effects | Duration |
|---------|---------|----------|
| **Clear** | Normal gameplay | 70% of time |
| **Rain** | Crops grow faster, fires extinguish | 2-4 hours |
| **Thunderstorm** | Lightning strikes, hostile mobs spawn | 1-2 hours |
| **Fog** | Reduced visibility | 1-3 hours |
| **Snow** (mountains) | Cold damage without warmth | Seasonal |
