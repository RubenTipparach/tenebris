# Venus - The Inferno

## Overview
Venus is Earth's evil twin - a hellish world of crushing pressure, acid rain, and surface temperatures hot enough to melt lead. But within its eternal cloud layer and in specialized floating habitats, strange and beautiful life thrives. This is the ultimate challenge for any space explorer.

**Radius:** 95 units (almost Earth-sized)
**Gravity:** 0.9x (nearly Earth-like)
**Atmosphere:** Ultra-thick CO2 + sulfuric acid clouds (EXTREME suit required)
**Day/Night Cycle:** Extremely slow (116 Earth days), retrograde rotation
**Water:** None liquid (too hot), acid rain instead

## Events

### Acid Storms
- **Frequency:** Every 10-20 game days
- **Effects:** Concentrated acid rain in cloud layer
- **Danger:** Damages exposed equipment and creatures
- **Protection:** Stay indoors, acid-proof coatings

### Super-Rotation Winds
- **Constant:** 100 m/s winds in upper atmosphere
- **Effects:** Platforms drift, navigation challenges
- **Use:** Can travel around planet quickly

### Lightning Storms
- **Frequency:** Common in certain zones
- **Effects:** Massive electrical discharge
- **Opportunity:** Harvest energy
- **Danger:** Direct strikes destroy structures

### Volcanic Eruptions
- **Frequency:** Rare
- **Effects:** New lava flows, resource redistribution
- **Danger:** Destroys surface structures
- **Opportunity:** Exposes rare minerals

### The Descent
- **Trigger:** Ancient ruins activated
- **Effects:** Path opens to deep Venus
- **Warning:** Things live down there that shouldn't exist...

## Travel

### From Earth
- Fuel required: 180 units
- Travel time: 45 seconds (game time)
- Challenge: Entering cloud layer safely

### From Venus
- To Earth: 150 fuel units
- To Moon: 200 fuel units
- To Mars: 250 fuel units

### Landing
- Cannot land on surface (instant death without extreme prep)
- Default landing: Cloud layer platform
- Surface expedition: Deploy from cloud base
